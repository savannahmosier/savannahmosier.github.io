How I Got My Name

As a child, my mother was always told by my grandmother to name her child Savannah because she loved the movie Savannah Smiles. My mom never liked the name but when it came to being pregnant with twins, my mother and father agreed on each picking a name. Ironically enough, my father was the one who chose the name Savannah.
